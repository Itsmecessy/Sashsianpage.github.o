Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

STEM education helps students develop critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills. It empowers learners to solve real-world problems and create innovative technologies.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

HUMSS covers human behavior, societal issues, and culture. It provides profound knowledge in politics, literature, arts, and society, honing communication and critical thinking skills.

Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

The ICT strand offers skills in information technology, preparing students for careers in tech-related fields under the TVL track of the K-12 curriculum.

Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM)

The EIM strand trains students in domestic refrigeration, air conditioning servicing, and electrical installation, ensuring readiness for hands-on technical work.

General Academic Strand (GAS)

GAS provides senior high school students exposure to diverse courses, helping them make informed decisions about their future career paths.
